ca.eandb.jmist.framework.scatter

Class ABMSurfaceScatterer

  • java.lang.Object
    • ca.eandb.jmist.framework.scatter.ABMSurfaceScatterer
  • All Implemented Interfaces:
    SurfaceScatterer, java.io.Serializable

    public final class ABMSurfaceScatterer
extends java.lang.Object
implements SurfaceScatterer
    A SurfaceScatterer implementing the ABM-U/ABM-B light transport models.

    The algorithm implemented here is described in:

    • G.V.G. Baranoski, Modeling the interaction of infrared raditaion (750 to 2500 nm) with bifacial and unifacial plant leaves, Remote Sensing of Environment 100:335-347, 2006.
    • G.V.G. Baranoski, D. Eng, An investigation on sieve and detour effects affecting the interaction of collimated and diffuse infrared radiation (750 to 2500 nm) with plant leaves, IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing 45(8):2593-2599, August 2007.
    Author:
    Brad Kimmel
    See Also:
    Run ABM-U Online, Run ABM-B Online, Serialized Form

        Parameters:
        x - The SurfacePointGeometry describing the geometry at the point on the surface to which the light is incident.
        v - The Vector3 indicating the incident direction.
        adjoint - A value indicating whether ray tracing is proceeding backwards (i.e., from the eye).
