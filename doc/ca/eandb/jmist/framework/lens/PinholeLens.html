    Lens, java.io.Serializable

    public final class PinholeLens
extends AbstractLens
    A camera that captures light at a single point. This is equivalent to the limit as the aperature width and shutter speed approach zero for a normal camera. A pinhole camera has an infinite depth of field (i.e., no depth of field effects are observed).
