    • Method Detail

      • rayAt

        ScatteredRay rayAt(Point2 p,
                 WavelengthPacket lambda,
                 Random rnd)
        Gets a ray indicating from which point and direction the camera is sensitive to incoming light at the specified point on its image plane. This will correspond to the direction to cast a ray in order to shade the specified point on the image plane.
        Parameters:
        p - The point on the image plane in normalized device coordinates (must fall within Box2.UNIT).
        Returns:
        The ray to cast for ray shading.
        See Also:
        Box2.UNIT

      • sample

        EyeNode sample(Point2 p,
             PathInfo pathInfo,
             double ru,
             double rv,
             double rj)
        Creates the terminal EyeNode for use by path-integration based rendering algorithms.
        Parameters:
        p - The point on the image plane in normalized device coordinates (must fall within Box2.UNIT).
        pathInfo - The PathInfo describing the context in which the path is being generated.
        ru - The first random variable (must be in [0, 1]).
        rv - The second random variable (must be in [0, 1]).
        rj - The third random variable (must be in [0, 1]).
        Returns:
        A new EyeNode.
