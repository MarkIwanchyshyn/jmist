        Parameters:
        p - The Point2 at the origin of the ray.
        q - The HPoint2 indicating the direction and limit of the ray. If q is a Point2, this creates a finite ray from p to q. If q is a Vector2, this creates an infinite ray from p in the direction indicated by q.
