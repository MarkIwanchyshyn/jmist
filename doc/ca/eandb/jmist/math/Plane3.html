      • altitude

        public double altitude(Point3 p)
        Computes the multiple of the normal that needs to be subtracted from the given Point3 to obtain a Point3 on this Plane3.
        Parameters:
        p - The Point3 to compute the altitude of.
        Returns:
        The multiple of the normal that needs to be subtracted from p to obtain a Point3 on this Plane3. This is the distance to p if p if on the side of this Plane3 toward which normal() points, or the negative of the distance to p if p is on the opposite side of this Plane3 toward which normal() points.
