      • scatter

        ScatteredRay scatter(SurfacePoint x,
                   Vector3 v,
                   boolean adjoint,
                   WavelengthPacket lambda,
                   double ru,
                   double rv,
                   double rj)
        Scatters a ray incident on this material.
        Parameters:
        x - The SurfacePoint that the ray is incident upon.
        v - The Vector3 indicating the direction of travel of the incident ray (note that the Vector3 points toward the surface).
        adjoint - A value indicating whether the path being simulated is in the reverse direction from the direction in which light is traveling (i.e., true for path tracing from the eye, false for light tracing).
        lambda - The WavelengthPacket associated with the light traveling along this path.
        ru - The first random variable (must be in [0, 1]).
        rv - The second random variable (must be in [0, 1]).
        rj - The third random variable (must be in [0, 1]).
        Returns:
        The ScatteredRay, or null to indicate that the ray was absorbed.

      • getScatteringPDF

        double getScatteringPDF(SurfacePoint x,
                      Vector3 in,
                      Vector3 out,
                      boolean adjoint,
                      WavelengthPacket lambda)
        Computes the marginal probability of emission in the specified direction.
        Parameters:
        x - The SurfacePoint for which to compute the emission probability density function (PDF).
        in - The Vector3 indicating the incident direction (note that this points toward the surface).
        out - The Vector3 indicating the outgoing direction for which to evaluate the PDF.
        adjoint - A value indicating whether the path being simulated is in the reverse direction from the direction in which light is traveling (i.e., true for path tracing from the eye, false for light tracing).
        lambda - The WavelengthPacket associated with the light traveling along the path.
        Returns:
        The marginal probability that the point x scatters in the direction out, given that light was incident from the direction in.
