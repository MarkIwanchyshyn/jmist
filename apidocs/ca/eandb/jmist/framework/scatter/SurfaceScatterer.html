      • scatter

        Vector3 scatter(SurfacePointGeometry x,
              Vector3 v,
              boolean adjoint,
              double wavelength,
              Random rnd)
        Simulates surface scattering.
        Parameters:
        x - The SurfacePointGeometry describing the geometry at the point on the surface to which the light is incident.
        v - The Vector3 indicating the incident direction.
        adjoint - A value indicating whether ray tracing is proceeding backwards (i.e., from the eye).
        wavelength - The wavelength of the incident light (in meters).
        rnd - The Random number generator to use.
        Returns:
        A Vector3 indicating the direction of scattering, or null if the ray is absorbed.
