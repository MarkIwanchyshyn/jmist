    abcx

      • lookMatrix

        public static AffineMatrix3 lookMatrix(Point3 eye,
                       Vector3 direction,
                       Vector3 up)
        Creates an AffineMatrix3 that transforms the standard view coordinate system to consist of the eye at the given coordinates looking in the specified direction. For the standard view coordinate system, the eye is at the origin looking along the negative z-axis. The positive y-axis points up and the positive x-axis points to the right.
        Parameters:
        eye - The Point3 indicating the transformed eye point.
        direction - The Vector3 indicating the direction to look.
        up - The Vector3 indicating the direction that is up. This vector will be projected onto the plane perpendicular to direction. This vector must not be parallel to direction.
        Returns:
        The AffineMatrix3 representing the transformation from the standard view coordinate system to the specified system.

      • lookAtMatrix

        public static AffineMatrix3 lookAtMatrix(Point3 eye,
                         Point3 target,
                         Vector3 up)
        Creates an AffineMatrix3 that transforms the standard view coordinate system to consist of the eye at the given coordinates looking at the specified point. For the standard view coordinate system, the eye is at the origin looking along the negative z-axis. The positive y-axis points up and the positive x-axis points to the right. This is equivalent to AffineMatrix3.lookMatrix(eye, eye.vectorTo(target), up).
        Parameters:
        eye - The Point3 indicating the transformed eye point.
        target - The Point3 indicating location to look at.
        up - The Vector3 indicating the direction that is up. This vector will be projected onto the plane perpendicular to the vector from eye to target. This vector (up) must not be parallel to the vector from eye to target.
        Returns:
        The AffineMatrix3 representing the transformation from the standard view coordinate system to the specified system.
        See Also:
        lookMatrix(Point3, Vector3, Vector3), Point3.vectorTo(Point3)
