        public short toCompactDirection()
        Converts this Vector3 to a compact, two byte representation of its direction. The first 8 bits represent the angle between this vector and the positive z-axis. The second (low order) 8 bits represent the counter clockwise angle about positive z-axis, with zero radians being the positive x-axis.
